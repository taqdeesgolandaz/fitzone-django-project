1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, purchase a membership, or contact us. This may include:

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Billing and payment information
  • Fitness goals and progress data
  • Workout and diet preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process your transactions and manage your membership
  • Send you technical notices and support messages
  • Communicate with you about products, services, and events

3. Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except:

  • With your consent
  • For payment processing through Razorpay
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

🔒 All payment information is encrypted using SSL technology.

5. Your Rights

To exercise these rights, please contact our support team.

🍪 By using our website, you consent to our use of cookies.
