1. Membership Refunds

Membership fees are generally non-refundable. However, we may consider refund requests on a case-by-case basis under exceptional circumstances.

💰 Refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of the original purchase date.

2. Free Trial

We offer a 7-day free trial on our Pro and Premium plans. You will not be charged during the trial period. If you cancel before the trial ends, no charges will apply.

🎯 Reminder: Cancel anytime during your trial to avoid being charged.

3. Cancellation Policy

You can cancel your membership at any time from your account settings. Cancellation will take effect at the end of your current billing cycle. No partial refunds will be issued for unused time.

4. Technical Issues

If you experience technical issues that prevent you from using our services, please contact our support team. We will work to resolve the issue promptly. Refunds may be considered if the issue cannot be resolved within a reasonable time.

🛠️ For technical support, email support@fitzone.com

5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact our support team at refunds@fitzone.com with your account information and reason for the refund request. We will review your request and respond within 5-7 business days.

📧 Please include your invoice number and order details for faster processing.

6. Payment Disputes

If you believe you were charged incorrectly, please contact us immediately. We will investigate and correct any billing errors promptly.

7. Chargebacks

If you initiate a chargeback without first contacting us, your account may be suspended. We will work with you to resolve any issues before resorting to account suspension.

8. Questions

If you have any questions about our refund policy, please contact us at support@fitzone.com
